Account Deletion Policy

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you can do so directly within the Shift Manager app.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Log in to your account in the Shift Manager app.
  2. Navigate to the Profile section.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm the deletion.

Data Deletion Details

When you delete your account:

  • All your personal information, including name, email, and profile details, will be permanently removed.
  • Shift logs and other data associated with your account will also be deleted.
  • Data required for legal or regulatory compliance may be retained for the necessary period, as per applicable laws.
